Sandwi recipe

Today we will explore the authentic sandwich recipe

An easy snack that anyboyd can do

Ingredients

  • bread
  • ham
  • cheese
  • lettuce
  1. take two slices of bread
  2. take two slices of ham and put them between the slices of bread
  3. take a slice of cheese and put inside the slices of bread next to the ham
  4. take the lettuce and chop it finely
  5. put the lettuce into the slices of bread
  6. enjoy

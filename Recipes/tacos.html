Tacos recipe

Today we will explore the authentic mexican tacos recipe

For many years tacos have been extremely popular in different countries but with every country having their own uniqu way to prepare them today we will explore the way mexicans enjoy their tacos.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  1. First start by finely chopping the meat almost to the size of minced meat
  2. Add salt and pepper to the beef and let it sit for 5 minutes
  3. Chop the lemons and have them reayd at hand
  4. Finely chop the onion coriander and mix them into a bowl
  5. add the beef to a hot pan with oil and cook it between 3/4 and well done
  6. Heat the tortillas in pan without oil until they feel soft and easy to handle
  7. add the beef to the tortillaand then add the cilantro/coriander mix
  8. Add salsa and lemon as desired
  9. Enjoy
